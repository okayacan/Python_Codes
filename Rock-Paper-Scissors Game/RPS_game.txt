It is presented a Python script for a simple command-line "Paper-Scissors-Rock" (also known as "Rock-Paper-Scissors") game you can play against the computer.
To play the game, you choose 'rock', 'paper', or 'scissors,' and the computer randomly selects its choice. 
The script then determines the winner and asks if you want to play another round.
There are many studies in literature in which equilibrium conditions of ecosystem are examined by implementing RPS-based models.
